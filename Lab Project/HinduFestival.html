    Holi

      HOLI

    Holi or Phagwah is a popular spring festival.

    Holi commemorates the slaying of the demoness Holika by Lord Vishnu's devotee Prahlad.

    Thus, the festival's name is derived from the Sanskrit words "Holika Dahanam", which literally mean "Holika's slaying".

    The festival is called Shigmo and Shimga in Goa and rural Maharashtra respectively.
